    [Find & Fix] Remediation Status (by Current Status) Represents the current stage of remediation actions raised after audits:
    Resolved: The remediation issue has been fully addressed and closed.
    Needs Evidence: The installer or responsible party must provide evidence that the remediation has been completed.
    Has Evidence: Evidence of remediation has been provided but is pending review or confirmation.
    No Access: Remediation could not be completed because access to the property was not possible.
    Overdue: The remediation deadline has passed without completion or sufficient evidence.
    Escalated to Scheme Provider: The remediation issue has been escalated to the relevant scheme provider for further action due to non-resolution or severity.    		 Term Assurance Find & Fix - EWI Performance Indicators dashboard
    [Find & Fix] % of Booked Audits Completed The percentage of booked audits that have been successfully completed with an onsite audit.
    Purpose: Measures the lag between booking and completion, showing how many scheduled audits have been fulfilled.    		 Metric Assurance (Number of completed onsite audits ÷ Number of booked audits) × 100 Find & Fix - EWI Performance Indicators dashboard
    [Find & Fix] % of In-Scope Properties Contacted The percentage of in-scope properties that have been contacted by TrustMark to offer an audit. Metric Assurance (Distinct contacted properties ÷ Total in-scope properties) × 100 Find & Fix - EWI Performance Indicators dashboard
    [Find & Fix] Audits Booked The number of in-scope properties where the household has confirmed and scheduled an onsite audit with TrustMark. Metric Assurance Find & Fix - EWI Performance Indicators dashboard
    [Find & Fix] Average Days to Resolved The average number of days taken to resolve a remediation case, calculated from the date the remediation was raised to the date it was marked as resolved.

    Purpose: Measures the efficiency of the remediation process and helps identify delays in closing compliance issues.    		 Metric Assurance Average of (Resolved Date – Raised Date) across all resolved remediations Find & Fix - EWI Performance Indicators dashboard
    [Find & Fix] Call Back The number of in-scope properties where the household requested or required a follow-up call to arrange an audit. Metric Assurance Find & Fix - EWI Performance Indicators dashboard
    [Find & Fix] CAT1 EWI Non-Compliance Rate (%) The percentage of completed audits where the EWI measure was found to have a Category 1 (CAT1) non-compliance issue.
    Purpose: Focuses on the most severe compliance failures for EWI installations.    		 Metric Assurance (Number of completed audits with CAT1 EWI non-compliance ÷ Total completed audits) × 100 Find & Fix - EWI Performance Indicators dashboard
    [Find & Fix] Complete Offer of Audit A household is considered to have received a complete offer of audit if either:
    The household has been contacted at least 3 times, and one attempt was via email OR,
    The household has accepted an audit.    		 Metric Assurance Find & Fix - EWI Performance Indicators dashboard
    [Find & Fix] Declined Audit The number of in-scope properties where the household refused the offer of an onsite audit. Metric Assurance Find & Fix - EWI Performance Indicators dashboard
    [Find & Fix] Do Not Contact Again The number of in-scope properties where the household explicitly requested no further contact regarding audits. Metric Assurance Find & Fix - EWI Performance Indicators dashboard
    [Find & Fix] EWI Non-Compliance Rate (%) The percentage of completed audits where the EWI measure was found to be non-compliant.
    Purpose: Highlights the quality and compliance performance of EWI installations after audits.    		 Metric Assurance (Number of completed audits with EWI non-compliance ÷ Total completed audits) × 100 Find & Fix - EWI Performance Indicators dashboard
    [Find & Fix] Households Contacted The distinct count of in-scope properties that have been contacted by TrustMark to offer an onsite audit. Metric Assurance Find & Fix - EWI Performance Indicators dashboard
    [Find & Fix] Incorrect Contact Details The number of in-scope properties where the provided contact information was invalid or inaccurate, preventing successful contact. Metric Assurance Find & Fix - EWI Performance Indicators dashboard
    [Find & Fix] In-Scope Projects Projects that meet all of the following criteria:
    Belong to ECO4 or GBIS schemes.
    Include an External Wall Insulation (EWI) measure.
    Have no previous onsite audit recorded.    		 Metric Assurance Find & Fix - EWI Performance Indicators dashboard
    [Find & Fix] Open Remediations The count of active remediation cases that are currently unresolved and remain open by UMR.
    Purpose: Tracks the volume of ongoing remediation work, helping monitor progress and outstanding compliance actions.    		 Metric Assurance Find & Fix - EWI Performance Indicators dashboard
    [Find & Fix] Open Remediations Overdue The count of active remediation cases that are still open and have exceeded their allowed timeframe for resolution:
    CAT1 Issues: Overdue if not remediated within 24 hours of being raised.
    Major and Minor Issues: Overdue if not remediated within 8 weeks of being raised.

    Purpose: Highlights remediation cases that are past their deadline, helping prioritise urgent follow-up actions.    		 Metric Assurance Find & Fix - EWI Performance Indicators dashboard
    [Find & Fix] Open Remediations Overdue % The percentage of raised remediations that are currently open and overdue.

    Purpose: Shows the proportion of remediation cases that are overdue compared to all raised cases, helping monitor compliance and escalation risk.    		 Metric Assurance (Open overdue remediations ÷ Total raised remediations) × 100 Find & Fix - EWI Performance Indicators dashboard
    [Find & Fix] Overall Audit Outcome (by Outcome) Represents the classification of completed audits based on their final result:
    PASS Audits: All EWI measures fully compliant
    CAT1 Audits: At least one Category 1 (critical) non-compliance identified
    Major Audits: Major non-compliance issues found (but not CAT1 level)
    Minor Audits: Only minor non-compliance issues identified
    Not Determined Audits: Compliance outcome could not be determined (e.g., incomplete data or inconclusive inspection)    		 Term Assurance Find & Fix - EWI Performance Indicators dashboard
    [Find & Fix] Remediation Raised The count of audits completed where a remediation report has been raised due to identified non-compliance or issues requiring corrective action.
    Purpose: Tracks how many audits result in formal remediation, highlighting areas needing follow-up and quality improvement.    		 Metric Assurance Find & Fix - EWI Performance Indicators dashboard
    [Find & Fix] Remediation Status (Overdue or Within Target) Indicates whether a remediation is within the allowed timeframe or overdue, based on severity:
    CAT1 Issues: Must be remediated within 24 hours of being raised.
    Major and Minor Issues: Must be remediated within 8 weeks of being raised.
    If the remediation exceeds these timeframes, it is classified as Overdue; otherwise, it is Within Target.

    Purpose: Helps monitor compliance with remediation deadlines and identify delays in corrective actions.    		 Term Assurance Find & Fix - EWI Performance Indicators dashboard
    [Find & Fix] Remediations Resolved in Time % The percentage of remediations resolved within the allowed timeframe, based on severity:
    CAT1 Issues: Resolved within 1 day (24 hours) of being raised.
    Major and Minor Issues: Resolved within 8 weeks of being raised.

    Purpose: Measures compliance with remediation deadlines and highlights process efficiency.    		 Metric Assurance (Number of remediations resolved within target ÷ Total resolved remediations) × 100 Find & Fix - EWI Performance Indicators dashboard
    [Find & Fix] Remediations Resolved Not Within Time %  The percentage of remediations resolved outside the allowed timeframe, based on severity:
    CAT1 Issues: Took longer than 1 day (24 hours) to resolve.
    Major and Minor Issues: Took longer than 8 weeks to resolve.

    Purpose: Highlights delays in remediation processes.    		 Metric Assurance (Number of remediations resolved outside target ÷ Total resolved remediations) × 100 Find & Fix - EWI Performance Indicators dashboard
    [Find & Fix] Sum of EWI Audit Measures Non-Compliance by Outcome The total sum of non-compliances for a measure, grouped by audit outcome category (CAT1, Major, Minor).
    Purpose: Helps identify the severity and distribution of non-compliance across different audit outcomes.    		 Metric Assurance Find & Fix - EWI Performance Indicators dashboard
    [Find & Fix] Total Contact Attempts The count of all attempts made by TrustMark to offer an onsite audit to in-scope properties. Includes every call or communication attempt, regardless of outcome (e.g., No Answer, Call Back, Declined Audit). Metric Assurance Find & Fix - EWI Performance Indicators dashboard
